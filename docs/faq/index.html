Faq

What is Judaism Online?

Judaism Online is a free educational site offering articles, classes and news about Jewish life and tradition.

Do I need any prior knowledge?

No prior background is required. Our beginner courses are designed for newcomers and those exploring conversion.

Is the content free?

Yes. All articles and courses currently published on Judaism Online are available at no cost.

Can I suggest a topic?

Absolutely! We welcome new ideas for articles and lessons. Use the contact form to send us your suggestions.

How often is new content added?

We post new blog entries weekly and regularly expand our course offerings throughout the year.

How do I enroll in a course?

Browse our course catalog and click the enrollment link on the course page. You'll receive email instructions for accessing lessons and tracking your progress.

Do you host live events?

Yes. We periodically offer webinars and virtual Q&A sessions with guest teachers. Event details are posted on our homepage and social media channels.
